Kakheti is a historical-geographical region in eastern Georgia. Kakheti is one of the big regions within Georgia.Kakheti is known for its many cultural monuments. Many churches,
monasteries and fortresses attract the attention of tourists and excursionists. The famous cultural monuments of Kakheti are: Ninotsminda, Bodbe,
Khornabuji, Ujarma, Sighnaghi, All Saints of Gurjaani, Alaverdi, New and Old Shuamta, Ikalto, Gremi, Necres. Each church-monastery is the bearer of an old tradition, their name-based holidays are still valid today.
This is the best factor for pilgrims or those interested in cultural tourism. The country has great potential for the development of wine tourism. The history of Kakheti winemaking c.
year It begins in the III-II centuries BC and is still the most important branch of the region's economy.
