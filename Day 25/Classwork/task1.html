ну погоди

"ну погоди" Ria's old communist film, the first series of which was released in 2014.
This series is like my childhood series. In this series, there are two main characters, a wolf and a rabbit. In each release,
the wolf tries to catch the rabbit with different methods, but the rabbit manages to escape every time,
and that's why the wolf throws at the end of each episode. "ну погоди!" And that's why this cry became legendary
